Converse County Library - Douglas


Location: 300 Walnut Street
Douglas, Wyoming
Building Size: Approximately 37,330 square feet; Two-stories with partial basement (new addition and existing building renovations)
Lead Designer: Hein - Bond LLC, Architects, Casper
Estimated Cost: $12,085,823 *
Current Schedule:
  • Architectural Design: March 2013 - January 2015
  • Construction Bidding Period: January - February 2015
  • Construction: April 2015 - July 2016
  • Occupancy: July 2016
Commentary:Architectural design was temporarily suspended in January 2014, to address a number of special design details affecting the Douglas Main Library. Since May 2014, the revised design is underway. Among the new accommodations included in the revised design effort is an expanded Genealogy area, as well as enhanced provisions for future floor space modifications within the library building. The design approach includes remodeling the existing library building and constructing an attached addition space to the north, as well as providing additional and enhanced parking area.

Arrangements for maintaining Library operations during the upcoming construction period are being evaluated by the Project Design Team.

* Estimated costs include construction and furnishings, architectural and engineering design, construction management, operations and maintenance, bond financing and contingency funding.
