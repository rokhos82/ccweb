Converse County Library – Douglas


Location: 300 Walnut Street
Douglas, Wyoming
Building Size: Approximately 37,330 square feet; Two-stories with partial basement (new addition and existing building renovations)
Lead Designer: Hein - Bond LLC, Architects, Casper
Estimated Cost: $16,853,641 *
Current Schedule:
  • Architectural Design: March 2013 - October 2014
  • Construction Bidding Period: Later 2014 (estimated)
  • Construction: Early 2015 (estimated)
  • Occupancy: Early 2016 (estimated)
Commentary:Architectural design was temporarily suspended in January 2014, to address a number of special design details affecting the Douglas Main Library. Prior to suspension, approximately 80% of the architectural design had been completed. Among the new design accommodations being evaluated is an expanded Genealogy area, as well as enhanced provisions for future floor space modifications within the library building. Arrangements for maintaining Library operations during the upcoming construction period are being evaluated by the Project team.

* Estimated costs include construction and furnishings, architectural and engineering design, construction management, operations and maintenance, bond financing and contingency funding.
