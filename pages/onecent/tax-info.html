In November 2012, the voters of Converse County elected to impose a One Percent Optional Sales Tax within the County to fund construction of three (3) specific public improvement projects to be completed in Converse County. These three projects are identified as follows (including the estimated project costs for each facility):

  • An enlarged Converse County Library in Douglas ($16,853,641);
  • An enlarged Glenrock Branch Library ($6,918,779); and,
  • A new Douglas Branch Campus of Eastern Wyoming College (originally $9,700,000; currently $10,579,735).

The Optional 1¢ Sales Tax is designed to remain in place until the full amount of tax revenues have been collected, at which time the Optional One-Cent Sales Tax will expire. The estimated term of the tax is four (4) years, with tax collections beginning in April 2013. Estimated costs for the individual 1¢ Projects include construction and furnishings, architectural and engineering design, construction management, operations and maintenance, bond financing and contingency funding.

The Converse County Commissioners are the owners of the three projects for the purposes of constructing the facilities. In May 2013, the County Commissioners selected Inberg-Miller Engineers, from Casper, to perform Construction Management/Owner's Representative services throughout the three construction projects.

The County Commissioners are considering funding the 1¢ Projects with public bonds, allowing construction of the improvements to proceed prior to collection of the full amount of the Optional 1¢ Sales Tax monies. The current construction schedules for the individual 1¢ Projects are projected as follows:

  • Converse County Library in Douglas
    • Architectural Design: March 2013 – October 2014 (estimated)
    • Construction Bidding Period: Later 2014 (estimated)
    • Construction: Early 2015 (estimated)
    • Occupancy: Early 2016
  • Glenrock Branch Library
    • Architectural Design: March 2013 - April 2014
    • Construction Bidding Period: May 2014
    • Construction: June 2014 – April 2015
    • Occupancy: May 2015
  • Douglas Branch Campus of Eastern Wyoming College
    • Architectural Design: January 2013 to August 2013
    • Construction Bidding & Award Period: August-December 2013
    • Construction: January 2014 – January 2015
    • Occupancy: February 2015
