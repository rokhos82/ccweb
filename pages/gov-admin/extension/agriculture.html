Agriculture and Natural Resources

UW Extension offers trainings to improve the skill of Wyoming's agriculturalists in managing their endeavors, contact the Extension Office to learn of local events. Services offered include: soil testing, water testing, yard calls, plant identification, hay and forage testing, pesticide safety training, grazing management consults, and rangeland monitoring consults.
