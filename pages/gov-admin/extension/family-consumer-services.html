Nutrition and Food Safety

UW Extension offers researched based programs and information for youth and adults in the areas of Food and Nutrition and Food Safety. Contact the Extension Office for local programs. Services offered include pressure canner dial gauge testing, USDA approved canning recipes, and local food resources.
