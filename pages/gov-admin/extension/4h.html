4-H

4-H is an informal, practical, learning-by-doing educational program for youth, age 8-18. The purpose of 4-H is to help youth acquire knowledge, develop life skills, and form attitudes that will enable them to become self-directing, productive members of society. Professional and volunteer staff provide educational projects and activities in animal science, home economics, natural resources, and handcrafts, as well as leadership and citizenship. Youth are involved in clubs and can choose from over 50 different project areas. More information on projects can be found on the University of Wyoming 4-H and Youth Website.

For current information, newsletters, scholarship instructions/application, and record books please go to the University of Wyoming/Converse County 4-H webpage  http://www.wyomingextension.org/converse4h/
