Official Duties

District Court is the Keeper of the permanent records. This office files and preserves all documents delivered for every court case in the Eighth Judicial District. Our records are from conception to current.

The District Court handles the following types of cases:

  • Civil
  • Criminal
  • Probate
  • Paternity
  • Juvenile
  • Adoption/Intermediary

We act as the Trial Court for the Supreme Court. We also act as the Appellate Court for courts of lesser jurisdiction (Circuit Court and Municipal Court) in Converse County. For additional information on a particular type of case please see case type section.

We are constantly striving to offer the latest improvements and updates that are available and we look forward to serving you with the best service available. I hope you will find the information on this web site helpful.
