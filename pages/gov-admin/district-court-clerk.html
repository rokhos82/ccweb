Official Duties

District Court is the Keeper of the permanent records. This office files and preserves all documents delivered for every court case in the Eighth Judicial District. Our records are from conception to current.

The District Court handles the following types of cases:

  • Civil
  • Criminal
  • Probate
  • Paternity
  • Juvenile
  • Adoption/Intermediary

We also act as the appellate court for courts of lesser jurisdiction (Circuit Court and Municipal Court) in Converse County For additional information on a particular type of case please see case type section We are constantly striving to offer the latest technology available and we look forward to assisting you with your filing needs.

IMPORTANT NOTICE: Please be advised that our staff is prohibited by state law from rendering legal advice, restating the law or recommending specific ways to pursue legal action. Nothing on this web site is intended to advise anyone as to legal remedies for a particular circumstance. Many matters of law are quite complex and may require special knowledge of legal principles and procedures.
