Wyoming State Fair Advisory Board

The County Commissioners appoint one (1) member to a four (4) year term, to represent the County. Members of the Wyoming State Fair Advisory Board must reside within the Board of Agriculture district he/she is to represent. For more information, please visit: wystatefair.com, or contact:
