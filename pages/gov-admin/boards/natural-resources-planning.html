Natural Resources Planning Board

The Natural Resources Planning Committee is a newly formed committee with seven (7) members appointed by the County Commissioners. There are particular qualifications for this board which include: involvement with and expertise in the various multiple uses of federal and state managed lands, as well as the customs, culture, economic viability, and social stability of Converse County. No person under the employ of any federal or state agency shall be eligible to serve. For more information, please contact:
