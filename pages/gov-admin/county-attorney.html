Responisibilities

The Converse County Attorney is an elected official, serving the people of Converse County. This office prosecutes violations of state law, both felony and misdemeanor, that occur anywhere in Converse County.

Requests for invesitgation should be made directly to those law enforecement agencies with jurisdiction.

The County Attorney is unable to assist the public criminal or civil matters, or provide legal advice.
