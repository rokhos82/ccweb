Goals of Emergency Management

Emergency Management focuses on analysis, planning, decision-making, and the assignment of available resources to mitigate, prepare for, respond to, and recover from the effects of all hazards. The goals of the Emergency Management are to save lives, prevent injuries, & protect property and the environment.
