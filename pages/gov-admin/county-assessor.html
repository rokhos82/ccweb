Statement

As Converse County Assessor, it is the responsibility of my office to discover, list and determine the fair market value of property within Converse County. To accomplish this, we use industry-accepted mass appraisal procedures and methodologies. Each year, items such as location, property characteristics, sales information, etc., are analyzed to determine the estimated fair market value. I believe that the cooperation and involvement of property owners contributes to a more accurate value.

Taxation is a complex and unpopular subject. Understanding the statutory requirements of this office and your rights as a property owner may help in relieving these concerns. I believe in your right to be an informed citizen. Providing you with information regarding all aspects of the assessment process is a priority. My staff and I are here to serve you. We are committed to providing fair treatment to all, starting with the manner in which you are greeted when you contact the office. We strive to ensure that the best available information is being used when determining the value of the property.

I look forward to any comments or suggestions that will allow us to continue to improve customer service and responsiveness.
