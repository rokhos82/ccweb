The Special Projects Department's responsibilities:
  • Updating the County's Subdivision Regulations
  • Processing subdivision applications
  • Coordinating projects with the County Commissioners and the County Planning & Zoning Commission
  • Coordinating updates to the Land Use Plan
  • Mapping projects
  • Issuing septic permits for small wastewater systems within the County as a delegated County to Department of Environmental Quality / Water Quality Division
  • Researching County Road histories
  • Writing grants for road reconstruction, building enhancements and building renovations
