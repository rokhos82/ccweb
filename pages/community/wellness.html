Our Mission:

We are dedicated to providing pertinent health information to the county's employees. In addition, the committee will provide wellness opportunities to increase knowledge about the relationship between lifestyle and health. Through these avenues, the employee will have the opportunity to take control of their health, increase moral and create a more healthy and positive working environment.
