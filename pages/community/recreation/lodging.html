Bed and Breakfast

Converse County is privileged to have two luxurious Bed and Breakfast hideaways!

Morton Mansion Bed & Breakfast

This stately 1903 Queen Anne Victorian home has been lovingly restored to turn of the century elegance. Relax on the old fashioned wrap around porch before retiring to one of three spacious bedrooms or a two-bedroom suite, all with private baths. Continental breakfast, reservations suggested, open year round.

Some credit cards accepted.

Guest Ranches

Cheyenne River Ranch
Don & Betty Pellatz
10301 Steinle Road
Douglas, WY 82633
(307) 358-2380
(307) 358-5796 (Fax)

An 8,000 acre working cattle and sheep ranch, family-owned and operated. Join us in our everyday activities such as fixing fence, moving cattle (June, August, and September), branding (June) and sheering sheep (April). Cattle drives available. Meals served family-style. Bed and Breakfast available. Three day minimum stay.

Deer Forks Ranch
Benny & Pauline Middleton
1200 Poison Lake Road
Douglas, WY 82633
(307) 358-2033

Fourth generation working ranch with two guest houses. Activities include roundups, branding, docking, trail rides, nature study and solitude. Licensed for big game outfitters, ranch recreation and Bed & Breakfast.

LaBonte Canyon Ranch, L.L.C.
Sheila & Duaine Hagen
831 Braae Road
Douglas, WY 82633
(307) 358-2447
(307) 358-3410

Beautiful family style guest ranch in the Laramie Mountains. Rustic & modern accommodations available. Fishing, horseback riding, four-wheel drive tours, hiking and quiet relaxation. Hunting also available with licensed outfitters. Breathtaking scenery and peacefulness are found here.

The Ogalalla Wyoming Cattle Ranch
Vern & Tami Moore
4095 Ross Road
Douglas, WY 82633
(307) 358-0161

Enjoy a true western experience on this working ranch. Featuring home grown/homemade family style meals, comfortable accommodations, hot tub, indoor heated pool, weight room, wagon trail rides, horseback riding and other ranch recreation.

Two Creek Ranch
Dennis & Nancy Daly
800 Esterbrook Road, Route 6
Douglas, WY 82633
(307) 358-3467

Working cattle ranch located eight miles south on Esterbrook Road. We have a 75 mile cattle drive twice a year in May and October. Guests may share our home or use the guest cabin. Activities include fishing, floating on the North Platte River, and rock hunting. The Oregon Trail goes though our leased property. Guest participation in ranch activities is encouraged.
