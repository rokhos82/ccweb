Notice is hereby given that Converse County, Wyoming will receive sealed Bids for road striping consisting of the following:

  • 368,000 Linear feet of Centerline Striping
  • 209,932 Linear feet of Edgeline Markings

The accepted quantities of each type of pavement striping will be paid per linear foot at the contract unit price.

A copy of the Contract Documents may be examined, or obtained from Converse County Road and Bridge, 10 Twin Bridges Rd. Douglas, Wyoming 82633, after May 21, 2014. For questions, call Converse County Road and Bridge at 307-358- 3602. The County roads to be striped are paved roads only throughout Converse County. Please examine a map of the County prior to submitting a bid.

This Project is funded through High Risk Rural Roads, Federal Project #HRRR 0.00 CN13071.

Bids must be received at the Converse County Clerk’s Office, 107 N 5th St., Suite 114 Douglas, WY 82633-2448 by 5:00 p.m. MST on June 13, 2014.
