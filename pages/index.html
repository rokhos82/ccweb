County Commissioners' October 6 & 7, 2015 Meeting Minutes have been posted. County Commissioners' October 20, 2015 Agenda has been posted. Invitation to Bid Snow Fence

Converse County is accepting sealed bids to build and install new and replacement snow fence panels and make repairs to standing panels as directed. Approximately 1000 panels will be built or repaired at predetermined locations. Locations are on Esterbrook Road (CR 5) at Esterbrook, Spring Canyon Road (CR11) near the intersection with Windy Ridge Road (CR 14), and Dull Center Road (CR 38).

Bids for repairs should be by board foot, and panels at per panel price. All panels will be built per Road & Bridge specifications. Site clean-up and removal of all old materials will be the responsibility of the successful bidder. Site clean-up is required before Converse County may be billed each month. All invoices or vouchers will be required at the Road and Bridge Headquarters two (2) working days prior to the County Clerk's office required date. All work is to be completed by June 15, 2016.

For panel specifications, locations or more information and bid sheets contact Converse County Road & Bridge at 10 Twin Bridges Road, Douglas, WY 82633, (307) 358-3602 or RoadandBridge@conversecountywy.gov

Sealed bids are to be submitted to the Converse County Clerk's Office at 107 N. 5th St., Ste. 114, Douglas, WY 82633 no later than 5:00 p.m. on the 15th day of October, 2015. Bids must be enclosed in a sealed envelope marked with the bidder's name, address, and Snow Fence Bid. Bids will be opened at approximately 8:30 am on the 20th day of October, 2015. Converse County reserves the right to waive informalities and to reject any and all bids.

More Information Converse County Launches an Unprecedented Platform

Converse County is bringing financial transparency to its citizens, launching a web site where they can browse the county's finances on-line with up-to-date financial information. The county has partnered with OpenGov to bring the citizens of Converse County the capability of reviewing all monies that are collected or disbursed by the county government.

Citizens and staff will be able to do a comparison over an eight-year span of revenue and expenditures using customizable graphs and bar charts. It can be as basic as finding out what the county's total revenue is, or as intricate as finding out what each specific department spends on office supplies.
