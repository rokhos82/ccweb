Request for Insurance Proposal

The Board of Commissioners of Converse County, Douglas, Wyoming requests proposals for insurance including: Commercial Property, Inland Marine, Auto Physical Damage, Crime, and Public Officials Bonding. Specifications can be obtained from the Converse County Clerk's Office or on the Converse County website. It should be noted that Converse County has obtained all of its liability coverage through the State of Wyoming, Local Government Liability Pool, and is not accepting bids for liability coverage.

Proposals are to be submitted to the Converse County Clerk, 107 N. 5th St, Suite 114, Douglas, WY 82633, by 5:00 p.m. on Friday, May 15, 2015 on the attached Bid Summary Sheet. Bids will be analyzed by County Staff and presented to the Board of Commissioners on Tuesday, June 2, 2015, or as soon thereafter as possible. Direct questions to Kim at 307-358-2244.

County Commissioners' January 20, 2015 Agenda has been posted. Converse County Launches an Unprecedented Platform

Converse County is bringing financial transparency to its citizens, launching a web site where they can browse the county's finances on-line with up-to-date financial information. The county has partnered with OpenGov to bring the citizens of Converse County the capability of reviewing all monies that are collected or disbursed by the county government.

Citizens and staff will be able to do a comparison over an eight-year span of revenue and expenditures using customizable graphs and bar charts. It can be as basic as finding out what the county's total revenue is, or as intricate as finding out what each specific department spends on office supplies.
