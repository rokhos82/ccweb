Department Information

 

Converse County established the GIS Department in 2006. The department is responsible for the development of the County's baseline digital spatial databases and for providing up to date mapping and data services to Converse County's departments, citizens, law enforcement and local government.

Other duties may include but are not limited to: overseeing the acquisition of new ariel photography, creating custom maps, maintaining and troubleshooting for various departments using GIS software, and remote data sensing.

The department is also responsible for the distribution of data, creation of new data layers, custom map generation, and analysing spatial data.
