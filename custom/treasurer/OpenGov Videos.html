WELCOME TO OPENGOV VIDEOS

Welcome to Converse County’s Financial Transparency site, where we encourage citizens to browse the county’s financial data in an interactive, engaging manner.  We have partnered with the government transparency experts at OpenGov to bring unprecedented access to review every dollar the county spends and receives.

Below we have compiled some video tutorials on working with OpenGov. The first video, Intro to OpenGov, covers the basic concept of what OpenGov is and how to access it. Following the intro is our second video, Working with OpenGov. This video explains more functions of the site, and how to move around and get to the information. The third and final video takes a much closer view at the Treasurer Office's own finances and shows you how to access specific details.

You can also get back to our main OpenGov webpage here.
