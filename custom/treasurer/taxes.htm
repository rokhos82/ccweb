Wyoming Property Taxes

Wyoming law delegates the task of collecting taxes to the county treasurer. Although it's hard to believe, in a lot of ways the collecting of taxes is a good thing. The taxes fund our hospital and schools, our health department, hospital and airport, and even help build our roads.

Unfortunately, the whole process of determining how much a taxpayer owes and where the money goes can be complicated. Hopefully, the information provided here can help shed some light on the process.
