PERSONALIZED PLATES
The Wyoming Department of Transportation issues personalized plates.

Personalized plates display the county designation and may have up to 4 letters or certain combinations of letters and numbers.

Due to the numbering system the state began using with the 2001 series plates, personalized plates having a combination of numbers and letters that begin with a number are not allowed. For example, MY65 is allowed while 65GT is not.

Personalized plates cost $30.00 and are good for the life of the plate series. The current series is scheduled to run from 2009 - 2017.

Once the Department of Transportation receives the application, it takes approximately 4-6 weeks for the plate to arrive at the County Treasurer's Office. The plate is always sent to the county designated by the plate prefix, meaning that any 13-XXXX plate would have to be issued by Converse County.

Personalized plates that say the same thing may be issued in different counties or on different vehicle types. There may be a 1-HUNT and a 13-HUNT as well as a HUNT for a trailer, truck, car and motorcycle.

WYDOT has updated multiple plate applications in October 2012. They are now easier to read and more streamlined. The state site has a little information about each plate type, plus downloads and online applications.

The Converse County Treasurer's Office can also print the forms out in our offices.

Veteran Stickers

WyDOT also issues stickers for Veterans who wish to show a specific license plate, but want more information about what they were involved in. These stickers are mailed directly to you, and cost $11.50 per vehicle.
